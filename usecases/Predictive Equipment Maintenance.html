Predictive Maintenance for Packaging

Monitor machine health in real-time to predict failures, automate spare parts ordering, and prevent costly line downtime.

Unplanned downtime stopping production?

Maintenance programs are often reactive or time-based. When critical filling or packaging equipment fails unexpectedly, the entire plant's throughput collapses.

  • Unplanned Stoppages: Failures in cappers, fillers, or wrappers halt the entire production line instantly.
  • Reactive Model: Time-based maintenance misses early warning signs of degradation.
  • Throughput Loss: Every minute of downtime results in permanent capacity loss and missed shipments.
  • Asset Degradation: Lack of early detection shortens the total lifespan of expensive capital equipment.
