Is equipment downtime killing your OEE?

Maintenance programs are often time-based or reactive. When critical packaged goods equipment fails, it causes entire plant throughput to collapse.

  • Unplanned Stoppage: Failures in capping, filling, or labeling stop the entire production line.
  • Reactive Maintenance: Waiting for things to break is expensive and inefficient.
  • Throughput Loss: Every minute of downtime directly reduces total plant output.
  • Shortened Lifespan: Failure to detect early issues leads to severe damage and reduced asset life.
