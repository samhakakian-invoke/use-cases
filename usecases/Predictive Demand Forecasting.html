Struggling with volatile demand cycles?

Consumer goods manufacturers operate in a volatile environment where traditional, backward-looking forecast models fail to align with real consumption signals.

  • Signal Misalignment: Traditional models miss real-time signals like weather, web sentiment, and retailer behavior.
  • Inventory Imbalance: Overproduction causes spoilage and high warehousing costs; underproduction leads to lost shelf space.
  • Operational Friction: Constant firefighting between sales, supply chain, and plant scheduling teams creates inefficiency.
  • Capital Waste: Inaccurate forecasting traps working capital in excess inventory or end-of-lifecycle write-downs.
