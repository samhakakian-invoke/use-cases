Predictive Maintenance for Filling, Packaging & Line Equipment

Description

Most consumer goods production losses stem from unplanned stoppages in packaging, filling, capping, labeling, sealing, and wrapping lines—making these assets critical to throughput.

Problem It Solves

Maintenance programs are still reactive or time-based. When a key piece of equipment fails, whole-plant output collapses.

How AI + Automation Changes the Outcome

AI monitors vibration, thermal readings, amperage, telemetry, and historical failure patterns to detect early signals of degradation. Automation generates work orders, orders spare parts, dispatches technicians, and adjusts production schedules instantly.

What Customers Can Expect

Higher asset uptime, more predictable production flow, and improved utilization across packaging lines.

Value

  • 20–50% reduction in unplanned downtime
  • 5–12% OEE improvement
  • 2–5 year extension of asset lifespan
