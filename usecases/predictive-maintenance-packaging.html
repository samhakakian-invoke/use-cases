Predictive Maintenance for Packaging & Line Equipment

Most consumer goods throughput losses originate from unplanned failures across filling, capping, sealing, labeling, wrapping, and packaging equipment. Traditional maintenance schedules are reactive or based on fixed intervals — not real asset health.

Problem It Solves

When a critical line component goes down, throughput collapses, orders back up, labor sits idle, and recovery costs compound.

How AI + Automation Changes the Outcome

AI analyzes vibration, thermal data, telemetry, bearing noise, pressure readings, and electrical signatures to detect degradation patterns before failure occurs. Automation creates work orders, schedules technicians, sources spare parts, and adjusts production sequencing automatically.

What Customers Can Expect

Higher OEE, predictable uptime, and more reliable packaging operations.

Value

  • 20–50% reduction in unplanned downtime
  • 5–12% OEE improvement
  • 2–5 year extension of asset lifespan
