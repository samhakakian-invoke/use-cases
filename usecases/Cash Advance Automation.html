Struggling with manual cash application?

Automating cash application has been an elusive objective for years. Your AR team manually matches payments from multiple sources to open invoices, spending hours reconciling accounts while cash sits unapplied. Complex payment scenarios and system limitations force time-consuming workarounds that delay cash posting and frustrate your team.

  • Manual Payment Matching: Staff spend hours each day manually matching incoming payments to open invoices across multiple systems and payment channels.
  • Multiple Data Sources: Payments arrive via wire transfer, ACH, credit card, checks, and lockbox—each requiring different handling and data extraction methods.
  • Complex Payment Scenarios: Partial payments, overpayments, underpayments, and multi-invoice payments create matching challenges that existing systems can't handle automatically.
  • Delayed Cash Posting: Manual processes mean cash sits unapplied for days, impacting working capital visibility and Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) metrics.
  • Time-Consuming Exceptions: When automated matching fails, staff must hunt through multiple systems to resolve discrepancies, consuming valuable time on low-value work.
  • Limited System Integration: Legacy cash application tools don't integrate in real-time with your ERP, creating data synchronization issues and requiring duplicate data entry.
