Is manual processing slowing down funding?

Running a factoring business can be messy and risky. Manual verifications, slow evaluations, and fraud risks create bottlenecks that frustrate customers and limit growth.

  • High Operational Risk: Manual checks on debtors and carriers leave gaps for fraud and compliance issues.
  • Slow Execution: Delays in front-end evaluation and back-end payments drive customers to competitors.
  • Inefficient Scaling: Increasing volume currently means increasing headcount and processing costs.
  • Process Friction: Customers need quick answers; manual validation slows down the purchasing process.
